About

I'm a Senior Multimedia Designer based in Guadalajara with 10+ years of experience across digital agencies, in-house teams, and broadcast media. I've designed brands, led campaigns, built content systems, and directed creative teams.

My work sits at the intersection of design craft and technical thinking. I'm comfortable in Adobe CC and equally comfortable writing Python to clean and analyze data or automate production tasks.

What makes me useful in a team: I think in systems. I don't just design a post — I design the template, the logic, and the workflow behind it.

Currently at SJRT (Jalisco TV, Radio & Noticias). Actively looking for in-house senior design roles at companies with a serious creative culture.
